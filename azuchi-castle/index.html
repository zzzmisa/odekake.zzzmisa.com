Azuchi Castle Ruins. This was the castle of famous feudal warlord Nobunaga Oda.

There are only stone walls and stairs were left, but I guess it is what is called decadentism, the place has a nice atmosphere.

From the tower ruin, you can overlook the townscape and the lakescape.
